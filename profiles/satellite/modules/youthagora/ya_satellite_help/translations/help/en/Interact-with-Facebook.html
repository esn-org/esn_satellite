Facebook is a popular social platform, and out of the box, Satellite provides you with two types of interaction:

  • Link your Satellite to your Facebook page or Facebook group in the Satellite Configuration page. A link to Facebook will be put on the homepage of the Satellite, in the "Follow us on..." block.
  • Allow users from Facebook to login to your Satellite, using Facebook Connect.This will require the setup and configuration of a module shipped within Satellite, and the creation of a developer application on Facebook. To learn how to do this, check out the video.
