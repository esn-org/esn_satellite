ESN Satellite is built on Drupal, a powerful open-source content management system. If you want to know more about why we believe Drupal is one of the most powerful systems to date, read the "What is Drupal" guide maintained by Acquia.

There are no limitations in administering your Satellite, and with over 4000+ modules available, you can easily extend the functionality of your Satellite. And with the new Plugin Manager, you can download and install modules from within your Satellite.
