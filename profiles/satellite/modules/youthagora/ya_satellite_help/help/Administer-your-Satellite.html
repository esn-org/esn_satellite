ESN Satellite is built on Drupal, a powerful open-source content management system. If you want to know more about why we believe Drupal is one of the most powerful systems to date, read the "What is Drupal" guide maintained by Acquia.

There are no limitations in administering your Satellite, and with over 4000 modules available, you can easily extend the functionality of your Satellite. And with the new Plugin Manager, you can download and install modules from within your Satellite.

Here are a couple of links if you want to learn more about Drupal:

  • Learn by the Drop: Getting started with Drupal
  • GotDrupal: An excellent source of videos guiding you through using Views, CCK and other Drupal modules
  • A guide for developing your own Drupal modules

And here some basic maintenance tips:

  • In order to backup your site it is not enough to copy all the files: configuration (and all site content) is stored in the database, so you will need to save both the files and the database.
  • It is important to periodically check for security updates of Drupal and the modules. You can do this through the Reports menu, but Drupal will do it automatically when you login. To upgrade Drupal, follow the guidelines at http://drupal.org/upgrade/; to upgrade modules, just remember that all contributed modules are in the profiles/satellite/modules subdirectory.
  • You might find Drush very handy if you have command-line access to your server.
