You can use external videos and images from providers such as YouTube, Blip.tv, Archive.org, Vimeo and Flickr, and many more.

To see a complete list of supported providers, check out the Embedded Media Field Configuration >> Providers.

To add external media to an article, an event, etc., you need to copy the URL of a video or photoset from the external provider (example: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4j9xgxrFkf4) and paste it in under Video when you are creating or editing a piece of content.
