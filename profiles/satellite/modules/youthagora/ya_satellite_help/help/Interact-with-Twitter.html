First of all, what is Twitter? Our suggestion: make a Twitter account for your section and keep in touch with people through "micro-blogging", announcing the latest news and changes in programmes on Twitter.

And guess what, you can also have interaction with Twitter on your Satellite, in two ways:

  • Link your Satellite to your Twitter account in the Configuration page. A link to your Twitter will be put on the homepage of the Satellite, in the "Follow us on..." block, so your visitors can easily track you down on Twitter.
  • Post every article, event, etc. directly on your Twitter account. This requires the setup and configuration of a Drupal module shipped with your Satellite. Check out this video if you want to learn how.
