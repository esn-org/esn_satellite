You can provide feedback when you are logged in to the website, for example, to make a suggestion on the page you are browsing, or to report a bug.

Your feedback will be sent to:

  • an internal feedback system managed under Reports > Feedback messages
  • to the mail addresses of all the users with the editor role on your website
  • to the ESN Satellite Google Groups, where it will be held under moderation. If your feedback message is relevant to the ESN Satellite community, it will be published, so the community can react on it

Internally, all those actions are managed by the Rules module. If you want, you can configure your own actions to be executed when a feedback message is posted (eg send it to the admin email address) by configuring the appropriate rules. Make sure to enable the "Rules Administration UI" module first.
